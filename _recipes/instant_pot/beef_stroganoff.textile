---
title: Instant Pot Beef Stroganoff
tags: beef main_dish instant_pot
---
* 1lb ground beef
* 1 small onion, chopped
* 3 cloves garlic, minced
* 8oz white button mushrooms, sliced
* 1 tbsp all-purpose flour
* 10.5oz can of cream of mushroom soup
* 2 1/4 cups low-sodium beef broth
* 8oz wide egg noodles
* 1/3 cup sour cream

# Set Instant Pot to Sauté. Brown beef.
# Add onion, garlic, and mushrooms, sauté for 1 minute.
# Add flour, soup, broth, and noodles. Stir to combine.
# Place lid on Instant Pot and set vent to Seal. Cook on High Pressure for 2 minutes.
# Release steam.
# Carefully remove lid and stir. Let rest for 5 minutes.
# Add sour cream and season to taste.
# Serve.
